Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

+

You can book a tour by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or emailing bookings@grouptraveladventures.com. We'll confirm availability and send you a booking form with payment details.

What is included in the tour price?

+

Most tours include accommodations, daily breakfast, some dinners, all transportation during the tour, professional guides, entrance fees, and a group travel coordinator. International flights are typically not included. Check individual tour pages for specific inclusions.

Do I need travel insurance?

+

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and baggage loss. We can provide recommendations for insurance providers.

Can I travel solo on a group tour?

+

Absolutely! Many of our travelers join tours solo. Our small group sizes make it easy to connect with fellow travelers, and we can arrange single accommodations (with a supplement fee) or match you with a roommate if preferred.

What is the cancellation policy?

+

Cancellation policies vary by tour and timing. Generally, cancellations 60+ days before departure receive a full refund minus deposit. Cancellations 30-60 days before receive 50% refund. Less than 30 days typically receive no refund, but travel insurance may cover this. Full details are provided at booking.
