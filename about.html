About Group Travel Adventures

Making group travel accessible, fun, and unforgettable

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Group Travel Adventures was born from a simple belief: travel is better when shared. What started as a small operation organizing trips for friends and family has grown into a trusted name in group travel, helping thousands of adventurers explore the world together.

Our founders, passionate travelers themselves, recognized that planning group trips can be overwhelming. From coordinating schedules to managing logistics, the challenges often prevent people from experiencing the joy of group travel. That's where we come in.

Today, we specialize in creating seamless, memorable group travel experiences that combine adventure, culture, and connection. Whether you're exploring ancient temples in Asia, witnessing wildlife in Africa, or discovering European capitals, we handle every detail so you can focus on making memories.

Our Mission

To make group travel accessible, enjoyable, and transformative by providing expertly planned experiences that bring people together, create lasting memories, and foster a deeper understanding of the world and each other.
